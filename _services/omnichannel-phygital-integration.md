Modern commerce doesn't happen in a vacuum. We bridge the gap between your physical inventory and digital touchpoints to create a seamless "Phygital" experience.

# Unified Commerce for the Modern Brand

Whether your customer is scrolling on TikTok, browsing in your flagship store, or searching on Amazon, their experience should be identical. We dismantle data silos to ensure your business operates as one unified entity.

### The Phygital Experience
* **BOPIS & BORIS:** Implementing robust "Buy Online, Pick Up In-Store" and "Buy Online, Return In-Store" workflows.
* **In-Store Digital Concierge:** Using QR codes and NFC tags to allow customers to access deep product specs, reviews, and extended digital-only inventory while standing in your physical aisle.
* **Unified Inventory:** A single source of truth for your stock levels, preventing overselling across marketplaces and physical locations.

## Social Commerce & Marketplaces

### TikTok & Instagram Shop Sync
We integrate your headless catalog directly with social platforms, enabling native checkout experiences that turn social engagement into immediate revenue without the user ever leaving the app.

### Marketplace Orchestration
Streamlining your presence on Amazon, eBay, and Walmart. We automate the flow of orders back into your central ERP, ensuring fulfillment is handled efficiently regardless of the sales channel.

> **Seamless Strategy:** We ensure that "Out of Stock" is a phrase of the past by balancing inventory across every node in your supply chain.

## Technical Stack
* **Headless POS Integration:** Connecting modern Point-of-Sale systems with your web frontend.
* **RFID & IoT Connectivity:** Tracking physical stock movement in real-time for hyper-accurate local availability.
